More Details

Your password passes through the server when you login, but is not stored permanently. It is held momentarily in a temporary variable, which is destroyed as soon as you finish logging in.

Your NetID alone is stored on the server for an extended amount of time. You are automatically logged out from Student Center if no activity occurs within 5 minutes. After this timeout your NetID is also removed from the server.
